Target

Target Weight drives your timeline (see Progress) — use a number you've actually hit before if you have one (most reliable, e.g. from a past scan). Target Body Fat % is just an expectation label. These two are independent and only saved as typed.

"Workouts/Week" and "Avg. Calories/Workout" here are only a fallback — once you log real workouts in the Workouts tab, the actual data is used everywhere instead. Your day-by-day burn, deficit, and projected weight change are tracked in the Calorie Tracker tab; your timeline and milestones are in Progress.
